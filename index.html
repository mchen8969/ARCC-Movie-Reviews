About

This site was created with the goal of making movie reviews easily available online. We want to improve access to these reviews for anyone with an interest in cinema, from casual movie goers to film buffs. Our goal is to share information on films and create a database of reviews for anyone trying to determine the quality of a given movie

Website created for CIS 181 at Harold Washington College using reviews sourced from IMDb
